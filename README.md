# Features & Walkthrough:


## On Startup:
When the program is first started, it will attempt to create necessary files needed for the system to run effectively. This takes around 10 seconds, and ‘Please wait….’ should be output.


<img width="590" height="89" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/78e15b8b-605f-4702-9790-37ffc11bf57e" />


## Main Menu:
Once you reach the main menu, the session information is displayed at the top of the output. Simply 
press the number corresponding to your desired action, followed by the ENTER key in order to proceed with your choice


<img width="942" height="381" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4fd6c79c-c130-4f29-aee9-fae0a9b0b6fb" />


## Create Account:
Press 1, followed by enter, to create an account from the main menu. Follow the on-screen directions to make an account. The name must be a valid string, and the ID Number must be a valid 8-digit integer, while the pin must be a valid, 4-digit integer. Please copy your Account Number and remember your PIN as well as the last four digits of your ID Number
### Important: For this step, a PIN and an 8-digit ID must be provided.


<img width="908" height="397" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b9929aa2-0619-4ed2-a74d-d9c0b11f418c" />



## Delete Account:
Press 2, followed by enter, to delete an account from the main menu. A list of all currently-available accounts will be produced and output by the program. Please type in the desired account number, followed by enter. Follow the on-screen instructions to delete an account.
### Important: For this step, the account number, PIN and last four digits of the ID provided during the creation process are needed.


<img width="949" height="820" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/180f37b2-a83e-4f38-b618-13de38e06384" />



## Deposit in Account:
Press 4, followed by enter, to enter the deposit function from the main menu. Follow the on-screen instructions to deposit an amount between RM0 and RM50000 into an account.
### Important: For this step, the account number and PIN and are needed.


<img width="942" height="381" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/05a1fb65-2081-4531-9683-6718cbec395b" />



## Withdraw from Account
Press 5, followed by enter, to enter the withdraw function from the main menu. Follow the on-screen instructions to withdraw an amount between RM0 and the account balance from an account.
### Important: For this step, the account number and PIN and are needed.


<img width="942" height="381" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/225cf8ee-ae2a-491b-8f84-94090222434d" />



## Remittance:
Press 5, followed by enter, to enter the remittance function. Follow the on-screen instructions to transfer an amount from one bank account to another. Please bear in mind that transfers between types of accounts (Savings and Current) will incur the following charges:


* Savings → Current: 2%
* Current → Savings: 3%
